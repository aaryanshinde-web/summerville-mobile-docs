## Profile & Preferences: Settings & Preferences

> Entry point is the Side Menu — tap **Settings** from the hamburger drawer to open the preference tree for account-level toggles, security settings, and display preferences.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Open the Side Menu

Tap the hamburger icon in the top-right of any screen to open the Side Menu. The drawer shows the member profile card (name, last logged in, **Switch profile** link), an **Enable alerts** master toggle at the top, and a list of menu items: **Settings**, **Alert Settings**, **Trust this Device**, **Apply for Loans**, **Statements & eNotices**, **Digital Wallet**, **Help**, **More Options**, **My Insights**, **Feedback**, and **Log out** at the bottom.

![Step 1: Open the Side Menu](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963761.png)

#### Step 2: Tap Settings

Tap **Settings** to open the account-level preferences area. This is where default accounts, nicknames, language, and other display-preference controls live. ### Summary

The Side Menu is the wayfinding home for everything that isn't a bottom-tab (Dashboard / Accounts / Move Money / Deposit). **Enable alerts** at the top is the master notification kill-switch — turning it off suppresses every alert category regardless of per-alert configuration, which is useful for members going off-grid briefly but usually not the right default. Settings below it is where the account-level preference tuning lives.

### Key Use Cases

* Member wants to change their default deposit account: Side Menu → Settings → Default Accounts.
* Member changing app language: Side Menu → Settings → Language.
* Member wants to kill all alerts temporarily: Side Menu → top-of-drawer Enable alerts toggle → Off.
