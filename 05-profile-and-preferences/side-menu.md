## Profile & Preferences: Side Menu

> The hamburger drawer that exposes every non-tab navigation — profile header, master alert toggle, Settings, Alert Settings, Device Trust, Loans, Statements, Wallet, Help, More Options, My Insights, Feedback, and Log out.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Open the Side Menu

Tap the hamburger icon in the top-right of any screen. The drawer slides in with three zones: the **profile header** at the top (member name, last-login timestamp, and **Switch profile** link to the persona switcher), the **Enable alerts** master toggle immediately below, and then the full navigation list. **Log out** is pinned at the bottom.

### Summary

The Side Menu is deliberately wide in scope — it's the catchall for every feature that doesn't earn a bottom-tab slot. Ordering is by member frequency: Settings and Alert Settings are at the top because they're the most-visited preference surfaces; My Insights and Feedback are near the bottom because they're lower-frequency. The profile header doubles as the entry to the business-context switcher, which is why **Switch profile** is surfaced there rather than buried in Settings.

### Key Use Cases

* Member switches from personal to business context mid-session: Side Menu → profile header → Switch profile → pick business.
* Member suppressing all alerts for a flight: Side Menu → Enable alerts toggle → Off.
* Member finding a deep feature like Statements: Side Menu is the one-stop map — tap the row, land on the feature.
