_Summerville Mobile › Profile & Preferences › Log Out_

## Profile & Preferences: Log Out

> Ending your session from the Side Menu — a confirm dialog prevents accidental taps.

**How to get here:** Side Menu (☰) → scroll to the bottom → **Log out**

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Tap Log Out, Confirm

From the bottom of the Side Menu, tap **Log out**. A dialog appears: *"Log Out? Are you sure you want to log out?"* with **Cancel** and **Log Out** buttons. Tap **Log Out** to end the session immediately — the app returns to the welcome screen. Tap **Cancel** to stay logged in.

![Step 1: Tap Log Out, Confirm](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776964245.png)

### Summary

Logging out is the explicit way to terminate your session before anyone else touches your phone. The confirm dialog is a simple safety check — accidental taps of Log out don't immediately end your session. On the next launch you'll need to enter your credentials and complete any device-trust step-up (OTP if you hadn't promoted this device to Trusted).

### Key Use Cases

* Handing your phone to someone (a friend, at a store): log out first so they can't see account data.
* Switching between profiles in a way that requires a full re-login: log out, log back in with the other member's credentials.
* Session timeout happened automatically but you want to be sure: log out manually to clear any cached state.
