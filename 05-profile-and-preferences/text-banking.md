## Profile & Preferences: Text Banking

> SMS-based banking for moments when you want balance or history info without opening the app. Enrollment is a single toggle plus a verified phone number, with a short command reference: SV HELP, SV BAL, SV BAL ALL, SV HIST, SV STOP.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Open the Side Menu

Tap the **☰** hamburger icon at the top-right of any screen.

![Step 1: Open the Side Menu](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963761.png)

#### Step 2: Tap More Options

In the Side Menu, tap **More Options**.

![Step 2: Tap More Options](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963761.png)

#### Step 3: Tap Text Banking

On the More Options screen, tap **Text banking** (the fifth row). The Text Banking screen loads.

![Step 3: Tap Text Banking](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776964011.png)

#### Step 4: Enroll in Text Banking

The screen shows the **Text banking** toggle (default Off with the status line *"Text banking is disabled"*), a **+ Add a new number** link, and a **Select a phone number for text banking** dropdown pre-populated with your on-file cellular numbers (e.g., *(+1) 224-698-9201*). Toggle on, pick a number, tap **Update** to enroll.

![Step 4: Enroll in Text Banking](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776964043.png)

#### Step 5: Review SMS Command Reference

Below the enrollment controls, the **Send A Text Command To 28640** section lists the supported commands: **SV HELP** (list commands), **SV BAL** (default account balance), **SV BAL ALL** (balances for all accounts), **SV HIST** (transaction history for default account), **SV STOP** (deactivate text banking). Text any of these to 28640 from the enrolled number; a reply arrives in a few seconds.

![Step 5: Review SMS Command Reference](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776964043.png)

### Summary

Text banking is the offline-safe channel — when the app is broken, the phone has no data, or you're on an old device, a quick SV BAL returns the balance via SMS. It's not a replacement for the app, it's a resilience feature. **SV STOP** is the most-asked support question — members sometimes opt in and then want out; the command works and self-service dis-enrolls without a call.

### Key Use Cases

* Member with spotty cellular data: SV BAL returns balance even when the app can't load.
* Member on a flight with SMS-only connectivity: SV HIST returns the last N transactions.
* Member wants to opt out: text SV STOP to 28640, or return to this screen and toggle off.
