## Profile & Preferences: Connect With Us — New Ticket

> The Connect With Us form for filing a written support request — pick an Inquiry type, attach files up to 20 MB, and tap Create. Routes the ticket to the right support queue based on the chosen type.

**How to get here:** Side Menu (☰) → **Help** → **+ Connect with us**

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Open the Side Menu

Tap the **☰** hamburger icon at the top-right of any screen.

![Step 1: Open the Side Menu](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963761.png)

#### Step 2: Tap Help

In the Side Menu, tap **Help — Support**.

![Step 2: Tap Help](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963761.png)

#### Step 3: Tap + Connect with us

On the Help screen, tap **+ Connect with us** at the top of the ticket list. The Connect With Us form opens.

![Step 3: Tap + Connect with us](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1777271985.png)

#### Step 4: Pick Inquiry Type

The form opens with **Inquiry type** at the top, defaulted to **General**. Tap the dropdown and pick one: **General**, **Account Inquiry**, **Transaction Inquiry**, **Bill Pay**, **Loan Inquiry**, **Travel Notification**, or **Feedback**.

![Step 4: Pick Inquiry Type](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963998.png)

#### Step 5: Attach Files and Create

Below the dropdown reads *"Each file must be smaller than 20 MB in size. You can attach documents, images or spreadsheets"* with a **Supported file types** link. Attach what's relevant and tap **Create** to submit.

![Step 5: Attach Files and Create](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963998.png)

### Summary

Inquiry type is the routing field — picking the specific category lands the ticket on the right queue and shortens response time, while General requires triage first. The 20 MB attachment limit covers most receipts, statements, and screenshots. Create commits the ticket and it appears in the Help screen ticket list with **Status: Open**.

### Key Use Cases

* Disputing a charge: **Inquiry type — Transaction Inquiry**, attach a receipt, **Create**.
* Filing a travel notice in writing: **Inquiry type — Travel Notification**, **Create**.
* Sending feedback or a feature request: **Inquiry type — Feedback**, **Create**.
* Asking about a loan balance or rate: **Inquiry type — Loan Inquiry**, **Create**.
