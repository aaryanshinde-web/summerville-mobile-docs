## Profile & Preferences: Calculators (Embedded Webview)

> A financial-calculators section that opens as an embedded webview into a Tyfone-hosted page — loan, mortgage, and savings calculators for members to plan without leaving the app.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Open Calculators Webview

From the Side Menu or Apply for Loans sub-menu, tap **Calculators**. The app opens an embedded webview to the Tyfone site (the page shows the Tyfone, harmoney, and Summerville nFinia branding alongside mock device previews and a **REQUEST A DEMO** button). A cookie-consent banner appears on first load: *"We value your privacy... Accept All / Customize / Reject All"* powered by CookieYes. Pick a consent option to dismiss and proceed.

![Step 1: Open Calculators Webview](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963949.png)

### Summary

Calculators is a third-party content surface, not a native feature — which is why the Tyfone marketing page header appears alongside the cookie banner. Members can still run loan and savings scenarios from here, but the experience is a webview, not a native screen, and the cookie banner is legally required on first load from the CMS. Dismissing the banner is a one-time action per device.

### Key Use Cases

* Member exploring a mortgage payment scenario: Side Menu → Calculators → pick mortgage calc, enter rate and term.
* Member running a savings compounding projection: Calculators → savings/compound interest calc.
* Member first-time access: accept or customize the cookie consent, then proceed to calculator list.
