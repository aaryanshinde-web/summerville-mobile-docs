_Summerville Mobile › Profile & Preferences › Calculators_

## Profile & Preferences: Calculators (Embedded Webview)

> A financial-calculators section that opens as an embedded webview into a Tyfone-hosted page — loan, mortgage, and savings calculators for planning without leaving the app.

**How to get here:** Side Menu (☰) → **Apply for Loans** → **Calculators**

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Open the Side Menu

Tap the **☰** hamburger icon at the top-right of any screen.

![Step 1: Open the Side Menu](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963761.png)

#### Step 2: Tap Apply for Loans

In the Side Menu, tap **Apply for Loans**.

![Step 2: Tap Apply for Loans](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963761.png)

#### Step 3: Tap Calculators

On the Apply for Loans screen, tap **Calculators** (first row). The webview opens.

![Step 3: Tap Calculators](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963943.png)

#### Step 4: Accept Cookie Consent and Use Calculators

The app opens an embedded webview to the Tyfone site (the page shows Tyfone, harmoney, and Summerville nFinia branding alongside mock device previews and a **REQUEST A DEMO** button). A cookie-consent banner appears on first load: *"We value your privacy... Accept All / Customize / Reject All"* powered by CookieYes. Pick a consent option to dismiss, then browse to the calculator you want.

![Step 4: Accept Cookie Consent and Use Calculators](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963949.png)

### Summary

Calculators is a third-party content surface, not a native feature — which is why the Tyfone marketing page header appears alongside the cookie banner. Members can still run loan and savings scenarios from here, but the experience is a webview, not a native screen, and the cookie banner is legally required on first load. Dismissing the banner is a one-time action per device.

### Key Use Cases

* Member exploring a mortgage payment scenario: Calculators → pick mortgage calc → enter rate and term.
* Member running a savings compounding projection: Calculators → compound interest / savings calculator.
* Member first-time access: accept or customize the cookie consent, then proceed.
