_Summerville Mobile › Move Money › Hub_

## Move Money: Hub (Landing Page)

> Every way to move money in the app starts here. Open the Move Money tab from the bottom navigation and you'll see all your options grouped into **Pay** (outbound one-off payments) and **Transfers** (account-to-account money movement). A **Quick Transfer** search at the bottom is a shortcut if you already know the recipient.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Open the Move Money Tab

From the bottom navigation, tap **Move Money**. The hub loads with the sections laid out as tiles.

**Pay** section (outbound payments):
- **Pay Bills** — traditional bill pay to a utility, card, landlord, etc.
- **Quick Pay** — single-sign-on shortcut into Quick Pay for one-off payments.
- **Zelle** — person-to-person payments by phone or email.

**Transfers** section (move money between accounts):
- **Other Members** — send to another Summerville member by their member number.
- **Internal Transfers** — move between your own Summerville accounts.
- **Same-day Transfers** — ACH same-day to an external account.
- **Send Instantly** — FedNow instant rail (marked **NEW**).
- **P2P Transfer** — external-transfer form (Send Money / Receive Money).
- **Manage Recipients** — view and edit saved external recipients.
- **Scheduled Transfers** — list of your upcoming scheduled transfers.

At the bottom, a **Quick Transfer** search field lets you type a recipient name to jump straight into a pre-filled transfer form.

### Summary

The Move Money tab is the mobile app's single shortcut into every payment rail — no nested menus, no hunting. The Pay / Transfers split matches how most members think about money movement: payments go **out to someone** (Pay), transfers happen **between accounts** (Transfers). The **NEW** badge on Send Instantly flags the FedNow rail, which is the newest capability and where most members will feel the biggest day-one improvement — it's instant, works on weekends, and goes to any FedNow-enabled recipient.

### Key Use Cases

* Paying rent via Zelle to a roommate: tap **Zelle** → recipient → amount → verify and send.
* Moving paycheck overflow into savings: tap **Internal Transfers** → From checking, To savings, amount.
* Sending a FedNow payment on a Sunday evening: tap **Send Instantly** → recipient → amount → confirm.
