# Move Money

_Summerville Mobile › Move Money_

> Every way the member can move money from inside the app — internal transfers, external transfers, P2P, FedNow instant, scheduled and recurring, Bill Pay, Zelle, Quick Pay, mobile check deposit, stop check, and skip-a-pay.

The Move Money Hub on the bottom-tab is the entry point; each row in the hub maps to a flow documented in this module.

## In this module

* **Move Money Hub** — the bottom-tab landing with all transfer + deposit options.
* **Transfer Funds — Internal & Other Members** — moving money between accounts inside the credit union.
* **FedNow Instant Transfer** — same-account or other-member instant transfer.
* **External & P2P Transfer** — sending money to an external account or another person.
* **Scheduled & Recurring Transfers** — setting up future-dated and recurring transfers.
* **Manage Recipients** — viewing the saved-recipient list.
* **Add Recipient** — adding a new external recipient.
* **Bill Pay — Add Biller** — onboarding a new biller for Bill Pay.
* **Zelle Send Money** — Zelle enrollment and send flow.
* **Quick Pay SSO** — single sign-on into the Quick Pay partner.
* **Mobile Check Deposit (RDC)** — capturing a check via the camera.
* **Stop Check Payment** — placing a stop on a written check.
* **Skip A Pay** — pushing a loan payment out by one cycle.
