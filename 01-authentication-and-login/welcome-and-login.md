## Authentication & Onboarding: Welcome & Login

> Every session starts here — the pre-login welcome surface and the credential entry screen are the first two places a member touches, and the login screen is also where account-level security alerts surface.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Welcome Screen (Pre-Login)

The pre-login landing greets a returning member by name and exposes the four most-used shortcuts — Accounts, Move Money, Check Deposit, and Manage Devices — without requiring full authentication. Tap **Log In** to proceed or **Enroll** if this is a new digital user; the **Balance** chip at the bottom is the one-tap balance-peek that works without a full session.

![Step 1: Welcome Screen (Pre-Login)](../.gitbook/assets/welcome-screen-prelogin.png)

#### Step 2: Log In With Security Alert Surfaced

The Log In screen is also where Summerville surfaces account-level alerts — in this case an **Unsuccessful login attempt** notification pinned above the Username field. Always read any alert before entering credentials; if the attempt wasn't the member, direct them to the fraud line (503-226-3939) before letting them proceed with login.

![Step 2: Log In With Security Alert Surfaced](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963035.png)

### Summary

The welcome and login pair balances speed with safety: quick actions and balance peek reduce friction for the 90% happy path, while the login screen actively surfaces security alerts and gives the member a self-service fraud phone number before they log in. Remember-me and fingerprint toggles are on this screen so members choose their persistence posture every time, and the "I need help logging in" link routes into the credential-recovery path without requiring a call.

### Key Use Cases

* Returning member checking a balance at a glance: tap **Balance** on the welcome screen — no login required.
* Member sees an unsuccessful-login banner on the Log In screen: confirm it wasn't them, call the fraud number listed in the alert, then reset credentials from "I need help logging in".
* New digital user: tap **Enroll** on the welcome screen and proceed through membership verification.
