# Authentication & Onboarding

_Summerville Mobile › Authentication & Onboarding_

> The first time a member opens Summerville Mobile, this is the journey: install, sign in with credentials, verify the phone, decide whether to trust the device, and land on the Home screen.

The pages in this module cover every screen between the Welcome screen and the first successful login on the dashboard.

## In this module

* **Welcome & Login** — the splash + sign-in form.
* **Phone Verification & Device Trust** — the OTP step on first login from a new device.
* **Trust This Device** — the prompt that decides whether OTP is required next time.
