# Business Banking

_Summerville Mobile › Business Banking_

> Everything inside the business profile — the Business Settings hub and the 12 sub-flows under Transfers, Manage, and More Options.

This module activates only when the member is signed in as a business admin (Profile Switcher → business profile). The Business Banking Hub is the map; every other doc here is one of its sub-flows.

## In this module

**Hub**

* Business Banking Hub — the Business Settings landing with all 12 actions.

**Transfers**

* Domestic Wire Transfer
* ACH Transfer
* P2P Transfer (Business)
* Transfer Templates

**Manage**

* Role Management
* User Management
* Approval Settings
* Recipient Management

**More Options**

* Approval Requests
* View Scheduled Transfers
* Business Contact Information
