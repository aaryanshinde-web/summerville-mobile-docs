# Add a Membership

_Summerville Mobile › Accounts › Add a Membership_

## Accounts: Add a Membership

> Members who hold more than one membership at the credit union — a personal account plus an LLC account, for example — attach them in-app so everything lives under one login.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Enter Membership Number

Enter the membership number the member wants to attach and tap **Next**. The screen is Step 1 of 2 — the second step validates ownership through core-system verification before the new membership is linked to the digital profile.

![Step 1: Enter Membership Number](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963718.png)

### Summary

Adding a membership is a verify-then-attach flow, not a self-service linkage — the core-system check in Step 2 is what prevents a bad actor from attaching a stranger's membership to their own login. Once attached, the new membership appears in the Accounts tab filter and in the Profile switcher, and any entitlements (signer authority, business admin role) flow through from the core record automatically.

### Key Use Cases

* Consumer member also opens a business membership: add the business membership number to see both sides in one app without switching logins.
* Signer added to a family member's account: the core flags them as a signer, and after adding the membership here they get the read/transact access the signer role allows.
* Wrong number entered: Step 2 will reject — re-enter from the Membership screen inside Profile.
