# Add a Membership

_Summerville Mobile › Accounts › Add a Membership_

## Accounts: Add a Membership

> Link a second Summerville membership (for example, a business LLC membership, a joint account with a family member) to your login so everything lives in one app. Start from Profile → Account Settings.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Open the Side Menu → Settings → Account Settings

From anywhere in the app, tap the **☰** hamburger icon to open the Side Menu. Tap **Settings** to open the preferences area. Inside Settings, open the **Account settings** tab (there's a **Membership settings** tab alongside it — both are relevant).

![Step 1: Open the Side Menu → Settings → Account Settings](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963761.png)

#### Step 2: Tap "Add an Existing Membership"

On the Account Settings screen, scroll to find the **Membership settings** tab. Inside, tap **+ Add an Existing membership** at the top. This opens the 2-step Add Membership wizard.

![Step 2: Tap "Add an Existing Membership"](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963710.png)

#### Step 3: Enter the Membership Number (Step 1 of 2)

The wizard asks you to *"Enter a membership to add to your account"* — type the membership number (e.g., *123456789*) in the input field and tap **Next**. The app validates the number against the core system in Step 2; you'll be asked to verify ownership through a short identity challenge before the new membership is attached.

![Step 3: Enter the Membership Number](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963718.png)

### Summary

Adding a membership is a verify-then-attach flow, not a self-service "any-number-works" linkage. The core-system check in Step 2 is the control that prevents someone from attaching a stranger's membership to their own login. Once attached, the new membership appears in the Accounts tab filter (**Group by membership**), in the Profile Switcher (if it's a business membership), and any entitlements (signer authority, business admin role) flow through from the core record automatically.

### Key Use Cases

* Consumer member who also has a business membership at Summerville: add the business membership number to see both in one app.
* Signer added to a family member's account: after the core flags them as a signer, add the membership here to get the access the role allows.
* Wrong number entered: Step 2 rejects the attempt — return to Step 1 and re-enter.
