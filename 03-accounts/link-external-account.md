# Link an External Account

_Summerville Mobile › Accounts › Link an External Account_

## Accounts: Link an External Account

> Add an account from another bank (Chase, Bank of America, your credit card, etc.) so it shows up in your Summerville accounts list and can be used as a transfer source or destination.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Find the External Accounts Section

Open the **Accounts** tab from the bottom navigation. Scroll past your Summerville holdings (Retail Checking, Retail Savings) to find the **External Accounts** section. You'll see a row of institution logos (US Bank, AmEx, Citi, Chase, USAA, etc.) and a **+ Add an Account** button. Tap the button.

![Step 1: Find the External Accounts Section](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776962804.png)

#### Step 2: Select Your Institution

The **Add Accounts** screen asks *"Select your institution"* with a search field and a list of suggestions (e.g., **MX Bank** shown). Type the bank or card issuer's name in the search box, or pick from the suggestion grid. This hands off to the account-aggregator (MX) for the actual connection.

![Step 2: Select Your Institution](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776962846.png)

### Summary

Linking an external account lets Summerville show you one unified view of your finances — your External Accounts contribute to the Dashboard Net Worth number and can be used as a transfer source or destination (picked up automatically in the Transfer Funds recipient picker). The connection is read-only — Summerville never moves money from the linked account without an explicit transfer you initiate. The aggregator (MX) handles the credential exchange and refresh; if an account stops syncing, the fix is usually to re-authenticate from this same screen.

### Key Use Cases

* Consolidate a checking-plus-savings setup where you use another bank: link both, see all balances on the Dashboard.
* Set up a regular transfer from an external account: link the external first, then it appears as a transfer source in Move Money.
* Credit card card-balance visibility: link the card issuer (Chase, AmEx, etc.) to see balances alongside your Summerville accounts.
