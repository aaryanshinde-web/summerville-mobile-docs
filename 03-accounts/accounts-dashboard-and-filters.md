## Accounts: Dashboard & Filters

> The Accounts tab is the member's daily-driver view — filters let them narrow to checking, savings, or a specific membership without leaving the screen.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Accounts List With Filter Panel

The Accounts screen groups holdings by account type by default and shows available balance alongside the masked member number for each account. Tap **Filter** to open the bottom sheet and narrow the view — filters include account type (Checking, Savings) and membership (useful for joint or business members with multiple memberships attached).

![Step 1: Accounts List With Filter Panel](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776962813.png)

### Summary

Filters are the difference between a clean daily view and noisy scrolling for members who hold multiple memberships or multiple account types — the filter selection persists for the session so a business admin filtering to their LLC memberships doesn't re-apply it every time they return to the tab. "Ask Penni" at the top of the screen is the virtual assistant entry point for balance and transaction questions, and **Group by account type** is the default grouping toggle that can be switched to group-by-membership when that's a more useful mental model.

### Key Use Cases

* Member with multiple joint memberships: Filter by membership to show only the accounts tied to a single member number.
* Business owner checking just their operating checking balance: Filter to Checking Accounts only.
* Clearing a stale filter: tap **Clear** in the filter sheet header to return to the unfiltered default view.
