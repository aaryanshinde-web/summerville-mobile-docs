# Accounts

_Summerville Mobile › Accounts_

> Everything that lives on the Accounts tab — the account list, per-account settings, beneficiaries, external account linkage, and account-closure requests. Use this module when the member is asking about a specific account on file.

## In this module

* **Accounts Dashboard & Filters** — the list view, group toggles, and filter sheet.
* **External Accounts** — viewing and linking external bank accounts.
* **Add a Membership** — adding a second member number under the same login.
* **Account Settings & Beneficiaries** — per-account preferences and read-only beneficiaries.
* **Request Account Closure** — submitting a close request through the app.
