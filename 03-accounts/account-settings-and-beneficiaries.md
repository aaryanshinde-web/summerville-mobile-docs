## Accounts: Account Settings & Beneficiaries

> View who owns each membership and who's listed as a beneficiary. The screen is read-only by design — beneficiary changes require a signed event, so the app shows you what's on file and routes changes through support.

### Step-by-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Open the Side Menu → Settings

From any screen, tap the **☰** hamburger icon at the top-right to open the Side Menu. Tap **Settings — Account and security settings**.

![Step 1: Open the Side Menu → Settings](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963761.png)

#### Step 2: Switch to the Membership Settings Tab

Inside Settings, two tabs are available at the top: **Account settings** and **Membership settings**. Tap **Membership settings** to see ownership and beneficiary information.

*(Capture of the tab row within Settings isn't in this set; the next step shows what the Membership settings view contains.)*

#### Step 3: Review Primary Members & Beneficiaries

The Membership settings view shows the **Ownership type** (Joint, Individual, Business), the **Primary members** list (e.g., *VICTOR CREED*), and a **Beneficiaries** section underneath. If no beneficiaries are on file, you'll see the line *"There are no beneficiaries associated with this membership — member number : 0000060071."* This view is read-only — changes require a branch visit or secure message.

![Step 3: Review Primary Members & Beneficiaries](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_1776963710.png)

### Summary

Account Settings is deliberately read-only on mobile for ownership and beneficiary information — these fields are frequent fraud targets and regulatory expectations require a signed change request, so the app shows you what's on file and asks you to contact support to update. The **Account settings** tab alongside Membership settings is where editable display preferences live (default account, nickname, delivery preferences).

### Key Use Cases

* Double-check beneficiary before a life event: Side Menu → Settings → Membership settings → confirm it matches your estate plan.
* Joint-ownership verification: confirm both joint members appear under **Primary members** before initiating a large transfer.
* Need to add or change a beneficiary: the app routes you to branch or secure-message — use Help → Connect With Us to start the request.
