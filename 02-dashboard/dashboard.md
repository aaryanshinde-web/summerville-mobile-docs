# Dashboard

_Summerville Mobile › Dashboard_

> Home & Insights is the screen the member lands on every time they open the app. The dashboard surfaces the highest-balance product on top, recent transactions, monthly cash-flow insights, and quick actions for transfers, deposits, and bill pay.

The pages in this module cover the dashboard view and the system-wide light/dark theme switch that affects every screen including this one.

## In this module

* **Home & Insights (Dashboard)** — the landing screen with balances, insights, and quick actions.
* **Light & Dark Theme** — the theme switcher in Settings that drives the dashboard's appearance.
